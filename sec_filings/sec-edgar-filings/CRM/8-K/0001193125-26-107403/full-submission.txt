<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><B>Salesforce Commences Largest-Ever $25 Billion Accelerated Share Repurchase </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman" ALIGN="center"><I>The historic transaction underscores leadership&#8217;s confidence in the company&#8217;s position in the Agentic Era and commitment to
driving shareholder value </I></P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>SAN FRANCISCO&#8212;March 16, 2026</B>&#8212;Salesforce (NYSE: CRM), the world&#8217;s #1 AI CRM, today commenced the
prepayment and initial delivery of approximately 103&nbsp;million shares under its previously announced<B> </B>$25&nbsp;billion accelerated share repurchase (&#8220;ASR&#8221;) agreements that it entered into on March&nbsp;11, 2026 with certain
financial institutions. This transaction, the largest ASR in history, represents the immediate execution of half of the $50&nbsp;billion aggregate Share Repurchase Program authorized by Salesforce&#8217;s Board of Directors in February 2026. </P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#8220;We are aggressively repurchasing shares because we are so confident in the future of Salesforce,&#8221; said Marc Benioff, Chair and CEO of Salesforce.
</P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">&#8220;This $25&nbsp;billion ASR reflects our increased conviction in the durability of our growth and cash flow trajectory,&#8221; said Robin
Washington, President and Chief Operating and Financial Officer of Salesforce. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:18pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman"><B>Transaction Details </B></P>
<P STYLE="margin-top:6pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">Salesforce has entered into ASR agreements with Banco Santander, S.A., Bank of America, N.A., Citibank, N.A., JPMorgan Chase Bank, National Association, and
Morgan Stanley&nbsp;&amp; Co. LLC, with J. Wood Capital Advisors LLC serving as an advisor. </P> <P STYLE="margin-top:12pt; margin-bottom:0pt; font-size:10pt; font-family:Times New Roman">The initial delivery of 103&nbsp;million shares represents
approximately 80% of the total shares anticipated to be repurchased, based on the closing price of Salesforce&#8217;s common stock on March&nbsp;11, 2026. The final number of shares to be repurchased will be determined generally by the
volume-weighted average price of Salesforce&#8217;s common stock during the term of the transaction, less a discount and subject to adjustments. Final settlement is expected to occur in the third or fourth quarter of Salesforce&#8217;s FY27. </P>
